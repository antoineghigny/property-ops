## PHASE 2: DEEP INVESTIGATIVE AUDIT

### A. Construction Rates Research (DYNAMIC)
- **Mandatory Web Search:** Find "average renovation cost m2 [Country] [Year]" for Refresh, Complete, and Structural renovation levels.

### B. Technical & Visual Audit (Expert Observation)
Analyze the listing photos (URLs) and report objectively on these 4 pillars:
1. **Structural State:** Identify if the interior is "bare bricks" (briques nues), "shell only" (gros œuvre), or "finished" (plafonné).
2. **Technicals:** Identify the age markers of the heating and electrical systems.
3. **Humidity:** Detect peeling paint, saltpetre, or dark baseboard stains.
4. **Consistency:** Check if the stated EPC/PEB matches the visual evidence (e.g., wooden single glazing vs PEB B).

### C. Context & Environment Research
- **Local Ecosystem:** Identify specific demand drivers (Employment hubs, infrastructure) within 5km.
- **Neighborhood Audit:** Document both nuisances (noise, industry) and growth drivers (gentrification, transport).
