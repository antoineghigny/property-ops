# Mode: screen

This is the default autonomous path for `/property-ops`. It follows the **EXPERT AUDIT PROTOCOL**.

## PHASE 1: PRE-FLIGHT INTERVIEW (MANDATORY)
Before any tool execution or research, the Agent **MUST** ask the user:
1. **Primary Intent:** Is this for living (Home) or for rent (Investment)?
2. **Strategy Preference:** If investment, is the user open to co-living (colocation) or only classic family rental?
3. **Budget/Cash Focus (Conditional):** If `securities > 0`, ask for:
   - **Liquidation:** Sell for cash.
   - **Nantissement:** Pledging titles for a standard mortgage (saves fees).
   - **Lombard:** Interest-only loan (Bullet) for max cash-flow.
   Do NOT ask this if they only have cash.

## PHASE 2: DEEP INVESTIGATIVE AUDIT
Only after receiving user answers, perform the following:

### A. Hyper-Local Market Search
- **Price Realism:** Minimum 3 parallel searches for realized sales (not just asking) in the neighborhood.
- **Specific Demand Audit:** Identify exact demand drivers (e.g., Biopark workers, BSCA crews, HELHa students) within 5km.
- **Micro-Location:** Proximity to Tram M3, noise corridors (BSCA), and future infrastructure projects.

### B. Regulatory & Risk Sweep (The "Surgical" Check)
- **Soil Pollution:** Systematic check of the BDES (Wallonia) or equivalent database.
- **Flood Risk:** Audit of WalOnMap for flood zones and water permeability.
- **Urban Planning (RU):** Verification of zoning (ZACC, residential, etc.) and potential for legal multi-unit conversion.

### C. Advanced Financial Modeling
- **Dual Scenario:** Systematically produce results for both 3% (Home) and 12.5% (Rent) duties.
- **Net-Net Yield:** Calculate cash-flow after 10% maintenance, 5% vacancy, and property tax.
- **Asset Leverage:** Model the use of securities (Lombard loan/pledging) as an alternative to liquidation.

## Reporting Standards: The Corporate Feasibility Study (Hotel-Level Analysis)
- **MASSIVE DEPTH:** Speed is irrelevant. Quality and extreme precision are mandatory. The final response MUST be exhaustive (1000+ words), detailing every facet of the local economy, transport links, environmental risks, and precise financial engineering based on the user's specific answers.
- **The "Asset-Liability" Bridge:** Clear, calculated distinction between mobilizable cash and total assets, including step-by-step pledging/Lombard strategies.
- **Negotiation Levers:** Minimum 3 highly technical points for price reduction (PEB, RU issues, market staleness, local competition).
- **Macro & Micro Context:** Cite actual companies, hospitals, and infrastructure projects found during your mandatory web searches. Do NOT use generic terms like "students" unless a major university is literally next door.
